Ushiromiya family

Main article: Ushiromiya family

Main family branch

Kinzo Ushiromiya (右代宮 金蔵, Ushiromiya Kinzō)
Kinzo is the aged head of the Ushiromiya family. A veteran of World War II, Kinzo came into a large quantity of gold while stationed in the Italian Social Republic. He used this gold to establish the Ushiromiya as a powerful family operating in several industries. In his later years, Kinzo had a mansion built on his private island Rokkenjima where he now lives. Every year the Ushiromiya family meets at the Rokkenjima mansion to reunite and discuss business matters. Due to Kinzo's age, the issue of inheritance is a regular topic at these family conferences.
Krauss Ushiromiya (右代宮 蔵臼, Ushiromiya Kurausu)
Krauss is Kinzo's firstborn son and the first in line to succeed Kinzo as head of the Ushiromiya family. His status as successor is contested by his younger sister Eva. Krauss is a fairly successful real estate investor, however he has recently come into a large amount of debt due to a resort deveopment.
Natsuhi Ushiromiya (右代宮 夏妃, Ushiromiya Natsuhi)
Natsuhi is Krauss's wife and Kinzo's daughter-in-law. As she is not part of the Ushiromiya bloodline, Natsuhi is not allowed to wear the family crest on her person, a fact that greatly distresses her. She is frequently bullied by Krauss's sister Eva who desires to undermine Natsuhi's position as head woman of the Ushiromiya family.
Lion Ushiromiya (右代宮 理御, Ushiromiya Rion)
Lion is Krauss and Natsuhi's adopted child and only appears in the miracle fragment. When Lion does appear, Lion takes on the burden of being the next in line for the Ushiromiya headship. Lion is said to be a near-flawless youth who the entire family finds worthy to be Kinzo's successor, even Krauss and Eva.
Jessica Ushiromiya (右代宮 朱志香, Ushiromiya Jeshika)
Krauss and Natsuhi's biological daughter. In most timelines, Jessica is burdened with being the next in line for the Ushiromiya headship after her father, a title Jessica does not want.

Second family branch

Eva Ushiromiya (右代宮 絵羽, Ushiromiya Eba)
Eva is Kinzo's second child. She frequently fights with her brother Krauss over being the successor to the Ushiromiya family, a title Eva strongly desires.
Hideyoshi Ushiromiya (右代宮 秀吉, Ushiromiya Hideyoshi)
Hideyoshi is Eva's husband and Kinzo's son-in-law. Hideyoshi is a very easygoing but successful businessman.
George Ushiromiya (右代宮 譲治, Ushiromiya Jōji)
George is Eva and Hideyoshi's only child and Kinzo's eldest grandchild. His mother Eva is especially proud of him as he originally was to be the successor to the headship after Krauss as Natsuhi was having trouble conceiving a child. However, after Jessica's birth, George was no longer the next choice for successor. George has developed an intimate relationship with one of the Ushiromiya servants, Shannon.

Third family branch

Rudolf Ushiromiya (右代宮 留弗夫, Ushiromiya Rudorufu)
Rudolf is Kinzo's third grandchild. Rudolf is a successful and notoriously ruthless businessman. His first child, Battler, was the result of his relationship with his first wife, Asumu Ushiromiya. After Asumu died in childbirth, Rudolf married his longtime friend and associate Kyrie.
Kyrie Ushiromiya (右代宮 霧江, Ushiromiya Kirie)
Kyrie is Rudolf's wife and Ange's biological mother.
Battler Ushiromiya (右代宮 戦人, Ushiromiya Batora)
Battler is Rudolf and Kyrie's first child and only son. Battler is the protagonist of Umineko no Naku Koro ni
Ange Ushiromiya (右代宮 縁寿, Ushiromiya Enje)
Ange is Rudolf and Kyrie's second child. Ange is almost always sick during the 1986 family conference and thus is often the only surviving member of the Ushiromiya family after the conference concludes. In the true timeline, Ange is adopted by the only survivor of the Rokkenjima explosion, her aunt Eva. The two grow to hate each other and Ange eventually leaves Eva's care to search for the truth regarding the destruction of Rokkenjima.

Fourth family branch

Rosa Ushiromiya (右代宮 楼座, Ushiromiya Rōza)
Rosa is Kinzo's fourth child and the younger sister of Krauss, Eva, and Rudolf. Rosa has yet to find a husband.
Maria Ushiromiya (右代宮 真里亞, Ushiromiya Maria)
Maria is Rosa's only daughter and the second-youngest member of the Ushiromiya family. Maria was born out of wedlock.

Servants

Genji Ronoue (呂ノ上 源次, Ronoue Genji)
Genji is the head butler to the Ushiromiya family and Kinzo's closest confidant. Due to his closeness to Kinzo, he is often seen as a spy by some of the other family members.
Chiyo Kumasawa (熊沢 チヨ, Kumasawa Chiyo)
Kumasawa is an elderly servant to the Ushiromiya family. She is known for her frequent gossip and love for mackerels.
Toshiro Gohda (郷田 俊朗, Gōda Toshirō)
Gohda is the head chef to the Ushiromiya family. He is the most recent addition to the servant staff, previously working at a well-known hotel. Since he was hired by Krauss and Natsuhi, Gohda is seen as more trustworthy than many of the other servants who are often seen as Kinzo's spies.
Shannon (紗音, Shanon)
Shannon is a servant to the Ushiromiya family and fiance to George Ushiromiya. Her relationship with George is often derided by Eva, who sees Shannon as being below George's caliber.
Kanon (嘉音)
Kanon is a servant to the Ushiromiya family. He has a crush on Jessica Ushiromiya though he feels as though he is unworthy of feeling an emotion such as love.

Other

Terumasa Nanjo (南條 輝正, Nanjō Terumasa)
Terumasa Nanjo is one of Kinzo's closest friends as well as his primary care physician.
Erika Furudo (古戸 ヱリカ, Furudo Erika)
Erika Furudo is a detective who washed up on Rokkenjima after falling from a cruise liner.
Beatrice Castiglioni (ベアトリーチェ・カスティリオーニ, Beatorīche Casutiriōni)
Beatrice Castiglioni is Kinzo's lover whom he met during World War II. Beatrice is the daughter of an Italian Social Republic official and was hidden in the Japanese military base near Rokkenjima, where Kinzo was stationed.
Beatrice Ushiromiya (右代宮 ベアトリーチェ, Ushiromiya Beatorīche)
Beatrice Ushiromiya is the daughter of Kinzo Ushiromiya and Beatrice Castiglioni. Kinzo, infatuated with her resemblance to his deceased lover, rapes and impregnates her.
Sayo Yasuda (安田紗代, Yasuda Sayo)
Sayo Yasuda is the incestuous child of Kinzo and his daughter, Beatrice Ushiromiya. In an attempt to hide Kinzo's actions, Genji offers Sayo to Natsuhi for adoptions. In almost every timeline, however, Natsuhi becomes greatly upset by the offer and accidentally pushes the servant holding the infant Sayo off of a cliff, resulting in the servant's death and the mutilation of Sayo. Sayo then winds up in the Fukuin House and trains to be a servant of the Ushiromiya family. She goes on to serve the family during the family conferences, taking on the two aliases Kanon and Shannon.
Sakutarou (さくたろう, Sakutarō)
Sakutarou is Maria Ushiromiya's imaginary friend.
